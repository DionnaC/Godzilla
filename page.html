Color and Font

Initially, I tried to match the colors of the pages to its content. For example, King Ghidorah and Rodan have colors matching their bodies. Color contrast is significant because text and background colors can determine how people interact with the content written in those colors. Low-contrast colors are challenging to read when text contrasts poorly with background colors, while high-contrast colors are easier to read when paired. I went through a few font choices, and at first had Lucida Handwriting for a while. However, the more I looked at my pages, the more that font seemed hard to read. I ended with a simplier font like Calibri.
